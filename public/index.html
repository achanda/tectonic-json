TexBench: A Unified Benchmarking Suite For Shifting Workloads

Welcome to TexBench, an LLM-powered benchmarking interface that allows users evaluate multiple key-value stores in parallel against standard benchmarks such as, YCSB, KVBench, db_bench, and Tectonic, along with dynamically shifting and user described custom workloads. To meet performance targets and minimize latency, benchmarking several data stores against expected workloads is often the only approach for selecting the most suitable one.
